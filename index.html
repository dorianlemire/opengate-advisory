About Us

Opening Gates.
Driving Growth.

Opengate Advisory is a senior-level sales advisory firm built for ambitious companies that want to grow in EMEA without the overhead of a full executive hire. We plug in at the strategic layer — as your Fractional CRO, your market-entry partner, or your on-the-ground business development lead.

With headquarters in Dubai and deep roots across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa, we bring the relationships, market intelligence, and execution discipline that take years to build — deployed immediately, at the pace your business demands.

Whether you're entering the region for the first time or scaling an existing footprint, Opengate delivers board-level strategy with hands-on sales execution.
